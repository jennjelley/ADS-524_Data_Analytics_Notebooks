Story 3: Call Center Inbound Calls

A call center receives an average of 3 inbound calls per 10 minutes.

Generate 500 samples from this Poisson distribution
In[45]:
lambda_value = 3
poisson_samples = np.random.poisson(lam=lambda_value, size=500)
Calculate the mean and variance
In[46]:
mean = np.mean(poisson_samples)
variance = np.var(poisson_samples)

print(f'\n Mean: {mean}' +
      f'\n Variance: {variance}')

 Mean: 3.034
 Variance: 3.188844
Interpret their significance for the call centers operations
In[47]:
plt.hist(poisson_samples, bins=10, edgecolor='black', alpha=0.7, density=True, label='Sample Data')

x = np.arange(0, max(poisson_samples) + 1)
pmf_values = poisson.pmf(x, mu=lambda_value)

plt.plot(x, pmf_values, marker='o', color='red', label='Theoretical PMF')

plt.title('Distribution of Call Center Inbound Calls')
plt.xlabel('Number of Calls per 10 Minutes')
plt.ylabel('Density')
plt.legend()
plt.show()
